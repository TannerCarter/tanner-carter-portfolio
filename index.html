About

My name is Tanner Carter, I am very musically inclined as well as tech savvy. I am a native to California and I am still, currently living here.
I took the UCLA bootcamp extension to learn various languages as well as framkeworks and widen my knowledge in the field to hopefully one day, become a full stack developer as a career.
Thus far it has been a learning experience, yet a fun one. To be able to create something from scratch and watch it come to life is pretty rad in my book.
